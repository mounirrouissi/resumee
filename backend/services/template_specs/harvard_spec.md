# Harvard CV Template Specification

## Overall Layout

### Page Settings
- **Page size**: A4 (210 × 297 mm) or US Letter (8.5 × 11 in)
- **Length**: 1 page preferred for ≤10 years experience; 2 pages acceptable for senior roles
- **Margins**: 20–25 mm (0.8–1.0 in) all around
- **Columns**: Single column main flow
- **Dividers**: Thin horizontal rule under major section headers (0.5–1px)

## Header (Top of Page)

### Name
- **Position**: Centered
- **Style**: Bold, underlined, serif (Times New Roman, Georgia, or Garamond)
- **Size**: 20–22 pt
- **Color**: Black (#000000)

### Contact Line
- **Position**: Directly under name, single line centered
- **Separators**: Small separators (• or |)
- **Content**: City, Country — Phone — Email — LinkedIn/GitHub/Portfolio
- **Font size**: 9–11 pt, regular weight
- **Example**: `Mounir R. • Tunis, Tunisia • +216 XX XXX XXX • m.m@example.com • linkedin.com/in/xxxx`

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Georgia or Garamond (serif) for formal look
- **Alternative**: Helvetica/Arial/Calibri for ATS safety

### Font Sizes
- **Name**: 20–22 pt bold
- **Section headers**: 11–13 pt bold, ALL CAPS
- **Body text**: 10–11 pt regular
- **Minimum**: 10 pt for readability

### Spacing
- **Line spacing**: 1.08–1.2 for body
- **Section spacing**: 6–8 pt space before/after section headers
- **Bullets**: Simple round or small dash; keep consistent

## Sections & Order

1. **(Optional) Professional Summary** - 1 sentence or bullet
2. **EDUCATION** - Reverse-chronological
3. **EXPERIENCE** - Reverse-chronological
4. **PROJECTS** - Company/Personal projects
5. **SKILLS** - Technical skills grouped
6. **PERSONAL INFORMATION** - 2–4 bullets max

## Entry Formatting Rules

### Education & Experience Format
```
Line 1: ORGANIZATION (bold, left) —— City, Country (right)
Line 2: Role/degree (italic) — YYYY – YYYY (right aligned)
Bullets: Each bullet ≤2 lines. Use 3–6 bullets per position
```

### Dates
- **Format**: Year-only (2019 – 2022) or month-year (Mar 2019 – Jun 2022)
- **Consistency**: Keep same format throughout document
- **Alignment**: Right-aligned

## Content Style Rules

### Tense
- **Current role**: Present tense
- **Previous roles**: Past tense

### Bullet Points
- **Start**: Strong action verb (Designed, Implemented, Reduced, Led, Automated)
- **Quantify**: Include metrics (%, $, time saved)
- **Tech stack**: In parentheses at end: `(Spring Boot, Kafka, PostgreSQL)`
- **Length**: ≤2 lines per bullet
- **Count**: 3–6 bullets per role

### Avoid
- Personal pronouns ("I")
- Long paragraphs
- Generic phrases ("responsible for")
- Images/icons that break ATS

## Visual Styling

### Section Headers
- **Style**: Bold, ALL CAPS
- **Rule**: Horizontal line across width
- **Spacing**: 6–8 pt above, 6 pt below

### Indentation
- **Bullets**: Indented ~6–8 mm from left margin
- **Bullet style**: Solid circle or small square

### Whitespace
- Generous white space between sections
- Clear visual hierarchy

## Colors & Graphics

### Colors
- **Text**: Black or dark gray (#000000 or #333333)
- **Accent**: Optional navy or dark teal for headings (minimal use)
- **Background**: White only

### Graphics
- **Avoid**: Background patterns, complex graphics, images
- **Keep**: Simple horizontal rules only

## ATS Best Practices

### Structure
- Use standard headings: EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, SKILLS
- No fancy multi-column layouts
- No text inside shapes/boxes
- Contact info as plain text (not in header/footer)

### Keywords
- Include relevant keywords from job description
- Natural placement in bullets/skills

### Format
- Selectable text (not images)
- Standard fonts
- Simple structure

## File Formats

### PDF (Primary)
- Embed fonts
- High-quality export
- Filename: `Lastname_Firstname_Role_Year.pdf`

### DOCX (ATS)
- No text boxes
- No headers with images
- Clean structure

## Accessibility

- Minimum font size 10 pt
- High contrast (text vs background)
- No color-only cues
- Print-friendly
